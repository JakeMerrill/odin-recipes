Stuffed Bell Peppers

Description

This is a rice, beans, and beef mixture that is stuffed inside of a bell pepper topped with chedar cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell pepper
  • 1lb in ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • 1 cup dry sticky rice
  • 1 1/3 cup of water
  • can of black beans
  • 1 cup of chedar cheese

Steps

  1. rinse rice in strainer until water comes out clear
  2. place rice and water in rice cooker and cook
  3. cook ground beef in medium-high heat until no pink
  4. sprinkle taco seasoning on beef
  5. place can of black beans in a pan and cook off most of the liquid
  6. combine rice,beef,and beans to make the mixture (prefferable in a large pan)
  7. cut off the tops of all 4 bell peppers and remove seeds
  8. stuff the peppers with mix
  9. place in oven for 10 minutes at 400 degrees
  10. take out of oven and put 1/4 cup of cheese on each bell pepper and put back in the oven for 2 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
