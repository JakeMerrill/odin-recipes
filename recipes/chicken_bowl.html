Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

Description

This dish might seem basic but it has stood the test of time through many cultures. It is a simple dish that features sushi rice topped with diced chicken as it core covered in sweet teriyaki sauce. To make this dish better you can add green onions with sesame seeds for some texture. This dish is relatively straight forward but will not disappoint is on the final product.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of uncooked sushi rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • teriyaki sauce
  • 2 chicken thighs
  • green onions
  • black sesame seeds

Steps

  1. wash rice with strainer until water comes out clear
  2. place washed rice into a rice cooker with 2 cups of water and let cook
  3. cook chicken in medium-high heat seasoned pan flipping every 3 1/2 minutes until 165 degrees
  4. once the chicken is done dice the chicken up into small cubes
  5. dice 2 strands of green onions
  6. Assemble
    1. Put rice in the bottom 3/4 of the bowl
    2. Place diced chicken on top
    3. place green onions with chicken
    4. pour teriyaki sauce on top and stir
    5. once stirred then add sesame seeds on top for texture
  7. Enjoy!
