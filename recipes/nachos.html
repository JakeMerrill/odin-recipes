Beef Nachos

Description

This is another simple but amazing dish. This dish uses your favorite type of tortilla chip. From here you add either chedar or mexican blend of cheese. Topped with ground beef with taco seasoning with green onions.

Ingredients

  • 200g of tortilla chips
  • 100g of mexican blend cheese
  • 200g 93/7% ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • green onions

Steps

  1. place ground beef in pan on medium-high heat
  2. apply taco seasoning to ground beef
  3. cook ground beef until no pink is shown
  4. strain ground beef
  5. place 200g of tortilla chips on plate
  6. place 100g of mexican blend and heat for 1min to melt cheese
  7. Place ground beef on top of melted cheese
  8. sprinkle green onions on top
  9. Enjoy!
